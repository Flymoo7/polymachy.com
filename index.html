About Us

Polymachy Ltd. was created by gamers, for gamers. Its two founders share a deep passion for immersive storytelling and a firm belief that rules and calculations should never get in the way of a great adventure.

Storytelling is fundamental to the human experience — from survival knowledge shared around ancient campfires to the social bonds forged through shared narrative. Today's TTRPG market is the natural evolution of that drive, transforming passive story consumption into active, collaborative world-building.

Even as AI reshapes industries, the appetite for authentic storytelling grows stronger. TTRPGs thrive because they speak to our desire to inhabit other worlds and create epic tales through human creativity and spontaneity.

Combat Copilot removes the mechanical friction that interrupts narrative flow — automating calculations, streamlining rules, and handling administrative overhead — so players spend less time in the rulebook and more time in the story.

Combat Copilot

Player feedback is clear — what gamers want from their character sheet is trusted, state-of-the-art data management with full ownership and portability. These five principles form the foundation of everything we've built.

01
Stability

Your character sheet stays consistent and reliable across sessions. Stats, history, and progress are always there when you sit down to play.

02
Security

Your data is protected. Nobody can tamper with your sheet, and your personal information isn't exposed or exploited. What's yours stays yours.

03
Portability

Take your character sheet anywhere — different devices, different platforms, different tables. No format lock-in, no dependency on a single service.

04
Ownership

You fully control your character sheet. No platform can delete it, monetise it, or hold it hostage behind a paywall or subscription.

05
Automation

The sheet does the heavy lifting. Modifiers calculate themselves, rolls are computed instantly — so you stay focused on the story, not the maths.
