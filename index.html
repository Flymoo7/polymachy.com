About Us

Polymachy Ltd. was created by gamers, for gamers. Its two founders share a deep passion for immersive storytelling and a firm belief that rules and calculations should never get in the way of a great adventure.

Storytelling is fundamental to the human experience — from survival knowledge shared around ancient campfires to the social bonds forged through shared narrative. Today's TTRPG market is the natural evolution of that drive, transforming passive story consumption into active, collaborative world-building.

Even as AI reshapes industries, the appetite for authentic storytelling grows stronger. TTRPGs thrive because they speak to our desire to inhabit other worlds and create epic tales through human creativity and spontaneity.

Our products remove the mechanical friction that interrupts narrative flow — automating calculations, streamlining rules, and handling administrative overhead — so players spend less time in the rulebook and more time in the story.

Multiple disciplines and talents — all coming together to deliver an unprecedented gameplay experience, incorporating

  • AAA game development
  • TTRPGs
  • Military and martial arts combat experience
  • Professional expertise in arms, armour and metallurgy
